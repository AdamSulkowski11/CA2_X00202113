About me

I am 24 years old originally from Poland and have lived in Ireland since 2005. I speak two languages fluently (English & Polish)

I decided to get into IT with the goal of learning more about tech and building a career in the IT field.
This comes from my time spent working a physically demanding job as a thermoforming machine operative in a plastic manufacturing factory.

I found my interest in IT at a young age. I have a vivid memory learning to use MS paint at four years old in front of a big boxy monitor.
During my younger teen years I developed an interest towards developing from spending time running game servers.

I enjoy puzzles and problem solving. I found enjoyment more so than frustration in the challenges that I have faced so far in my experience with IT which always bring a great sense of achievement once solved.

This is also further strengthened from my enjoyment of working on cars and my previous factory jobs, which both scenarios always provided a physical problem to solve.
